Television energy consumption

TV electricity use depends on screen size, panel type, brightness, and usage patterns.

Typical annual energy use (illustrative)

  • 32" LED TV: ~60–80 kWh/year
  • 55" 4K LED TV: ~120–180 kWh/year
  • 65" 4K OLED TV: ~150–220 kWh/year

Actual consumption varies by model and settings. Check the Australian Energy Rating Label for the specific kWh figure.

Tips to reduce TV energy use

  • Enable energy-saving or ambient light modes.
  • Reduce peak brightness for night viewing.
  • Turn off at the wall to avoid standby draw when not in use.
