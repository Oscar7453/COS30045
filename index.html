Appliance energy use in Australia

Explore typical energy consumption of household appliances and learn how efficient choices reduce costs and emissions.

Why energy ratings matter

The Australian Energy Rating Label helps you compare running costs. More stars means less electricity used. Choosing a more efficient TV can save households up to dozens of dollars per year depending on screen size and usage.

  • Look for the star rating and annual kWh figure.
  • Consider screen size, brightness, and standby consumption.
  • LED/LCD and OLED panels vary in efficiency by model and settings.
